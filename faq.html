Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

Product & Shipment

You can check product availability directly on our website. Simply search for the item you’re interested in and select your nearest IKEA store from the list of locations. The availability will be displayed on the product page.

Yes, IKEA offers home delivery services for online purchases. After placing your order, you can choose between standard delivery or express delivery, depending on your location. Shipping fees apply based on your order and delivery method.

If a product is out of stock, you can sign up for an email notification on the product page, and we will notify you when it’s back in stock. Alternatively, you can visit your nearest IKEA store to see if it is available there.

Payment

We accept the following payment methods: Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express IKEA Gift Cards PayPal Apple Pay (for mobile app purchases) Bank Transfer (for large orders in select countries) Please note that availability of payment methods may vary depending on your location and the platform (online or in-store).

Yes, IKEA offers flexible financing options through third-party providers in select regions. You can apply for installment payments at checkout for eligible purchases. Please check the financing option terms and conditions for further details.

Yes, IKEA uses the latest encryption technologies to ensure that your payment and personal details are secure. All transactions are processed through a secure and encrypted payment gateway, and we do not store your credit card information.
